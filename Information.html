TUWC Fall 2020 Points List

Activity

Point Value

Rowing/Running Cardio/SS Erging or running where your heart rate is ideally between 140-160 HR +1.5 point per 20 minutes
Ex: if you did 60 minutes of erging that would be 4.5 points
Bike/Other Cardio Lower impact cardio +1 per 20 minutes
Ex. if you went on a 1 hour bike that would be 3 points
AT Workout Anaerobic threshold at the point between aerobic and anaerobic levels Generally rate capped pieces Stadiums high intensity spinning on bike +2 points per 10 minutes (not including rest)
Ex.if you did 5x5’ w/ 3’ rest that would be 5 points (you would log 25 min)
Sprint/TR Workout Short highest intensity pieces Short burst high sprints on erg/bike/ running +2.5 points per 10 min (not including rest)
Ex. if you did 20 x 1’ on 1’ off that would be 5 points (and you would log 20 min)
Lift or more relaxed Circuits Lifts from Michael Workouts with more than 20’ rest for every 60’ Workouts where your heart rate is @ss or lower than ss +1.5 points per 20 minutes Ex. if you did a 60 minute lift that would be 4.5 points
HIIT Circuit Workout with maximum rest time of 20’ for every 60’... this is max rest time of 40’ on 20’ off Berries and Cherries 50’ on 10’ off 45’ on 15’ off +2 points per 20 minutes (including rest)
Ex. if you did 60’ of B’s and C’s you would get 6 points
Stretching (at least 10 minutes) +1 point
Core (at least 10 minutes) +1 point
Yoga +0.5 points per 20 minutes
Ex. if you did 40 minutes of yoga,that would be 1 point
